## **Page Strategy: Two Audiences, One Activation**

A Headshot Activation™ is NOT a headshot session. It is a professional engagement system deployed at live events. This page must clearly communicate that distinction while serving two different buyers:

**Audience 1: The Exhibitor / Field Marketer (Primary)** They're investing in a trade show booth and need something that drives real traffic, captures leads, and gives their sales team an advantage. They're thinking about ROI, engagement, and booth performance. This maps to ICP \#3: The ROI Operator.

**Audience 2: The Event Host / Corporate Organizer (Secondary)** They're hosting a conference, corporate event, or tenant appreciation program and want to offer attendees something genuinely valuable — professional headshot updates as a benefit. They're thinking about attendee experience, brand elevation, and "wow factor." This maps to ICP \#2: The Internal Owner and ICP \#1: The External Operator.

**The Cushman Wakefield Model:** A property management company or corporate host books JHR to provide headshot updates for their tenants or employees as a value-add. This is a retention and relationship play, not a lead gen play. The FAQ section addresses this use case directly.

**Programmatic SEO / GEO Strategy:** This page will eventually link out to headshot-style landing pages (Corporate Headshots, Lawyer Headshots, Banker Headshots, Casual Professional Headshots, etc.). These links appear naturally in the content and FAQ section, establishing the hub-and-spoke architecture without forcing it. Those pages don't need to exist yet — the link structure is planted now for when they're built.

## **SECTION 9: FAQ — Headshot Activation**

These FAQs are specific to the Headshot Activation service page. They address real questions from both audiences, clear up the "activation vs. headshot session" confusion, and plant natural links to programmatic pages.

### **Copy**

**\[Eyebrow Text\]** Common Questions

**\[H2 Heading\]** Headshot Activation FAQs

---

**Q: What's the difference between a Headshot Activation and a regular headshot session?**

A: A Headshot Activation is a professional engagement system designed for live events — trade shows, conferences, and corporate programs. It's not a studio session. We deploy a complete setup at your event: professional lighting, a Camera Ready Touchup Service, live image selection, and direct-to-attendee delivery. The goal isn't just headshots — it's booth traffic, attendee engagement, and lead capture, all wrapped in a professional experience people genuinely enjoy. If you're looking for dedicated headshots for your leadership team or small group in a controlled setting, that's our [Executive Imaging™](https://claude.ai/services/corporate-headshot-program) service.

---

**Q: How many headshots can you handle in a day?**

A: A single operator can comfortably handle 100–150 attendees in a full-day activation, depending on the flow of your event. For higher-volume events, we add operators to increase throughput without sacrificing the quality of the experience. We'll discuss expected attendance during our planning conversation and recommend the right setup.

---

**Q: Do attendees need to schedule a time slot?**

A: It depends on the format. For trade show activations, we typically run open-flow — attendees walk up when they're ready, which keeps the booth energy high and the line moving. For corporate events or tenant programs where you want every attendee to participate, we can set up scheduled time slots to ensure smooth rotation and full coverage. We'll recommend the best approach based on your event format.

---

**Q: What headshot styles do you offer within an activation?**

A: Our operators tailor the approach to each attendee based on their industry and professional needs. Common styles include [corporate headshots](https://claude.ai/headshots/corporate-headshots), [legal and finance headshots](https://claude.ai/headshots/lawyer-headshots), [casual professional headshots](https://claude.ai/headshots/casual-professional-headshots), and [executive portraits](https://claude.ai/headshots/executive-headshots). The beauty of a live activation is that attendees across different industries can each get a result that fits their personal brand — all within the same event.

---

**Q: How fast do attendees receive their headshots?**

A: Most attendees receive their polished, retouched headshot within minutes of their session. We use a tethered workflow with live selection — attendees choose their favorite image on the spot, and it's delivered directly to them via email or text. No waiting days for proofs.

---

**Q: What does the Camera Ready Touchup Service include?**

A: A professional hair and makeup touchup artist is on-site for the full activation. Every attendee gets a quick touchup before stepping in front of the camera — it takes about two minutes and dramatically increases confidence and quality of results. This is included with every Headshot Activation. It's one of the reasons attendees line up — it makes the whole experience feel premium.

---

**Q: Can we brand the activation with our company's identity?**

A: Yes — every touchpoint is white-labeled to your brand. The delivery gallery, the email notifications, and image overlays can all carry your logo and brand colors. For exhibitors, this means every headshot your attendees share on LinkedIn carries your brand with it. That's organic reach you didn't have to pay for.

---

**Q: We're hosting a corporate event and want to offer headshots as an attendee benefit — not for lead capture. Does this still work?**

A: Absolutely. This is one of the most popular ways organizations use Headshot Activations outside of trade shows. Companies like property management firms, corporate campuses, and large employers book activations as a premium benefit for tenants, employees, or conference attendees. The setup is the same — professional lighting, Camera Ready Touchup Service, direct delivery — but the focus shifts from lead capture to attendee value and relationship building. It's one of those offerings attendees genuinely thank you for.

---

**Q: How much space does the activation require?**

A: A standard Headshot Activation setup requires approximately a 10x10 foot area — roughly the size of a standard trade show booth. For larger activations with multiple operators or a dedicated Camera Ready Touchup station, we may need a 10x20 space. We'll discuss layout during planning and can work within your existing booth footprint or event floor plan.

---

**Q: Do you handle the setup and teardown?**

A: Yes. We arrive early, set up everything, run the activation for the full engagement period, and tear down at the end. You don't need to provide anything beyond the space and power access. We handle the rest.

---

**Q: Can you integrate with our CRM or lead capture system?**

A: Yes. We can integrate lead capture data with most standard CRM platforms and event lead retrieval systems. During planning, we'll confirm the integration method that works best for your setup — whether that's direct API integration, CSV export, or real-time data delivery to your sales team on the floor.

---

**Q: How far in advance should we book?**

A: We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks before your event to ensure operator availability and allow time for branding coordination. For large conventions or multi-day activations, 8–12 weeks is ideal. That said, if your event is sooner, reach out anyway — we'll do our best to accommodate.
