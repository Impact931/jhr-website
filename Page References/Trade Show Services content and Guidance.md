## **Page Strategy: Three Audiences, One Show Floor**

Trade-Show Media Services™ is NOT the same as Corporate Event Coverage™. Corporate Event Coverage documents the overall event — keynotes, general sessions, the arc of a conference. Trade-Show Media Services captures the energy, brand presence, and stakeholder engagement happening across the show floor — booths, sponsors, product displays, demonstrations, and the genuine interactions that drive business.

This page must clearly communicate that distinction while serving three different buyers:

**Audience 1: The Event Host / Show Organizer (Primary)** They're producing the trade show or industry expo. They need media that elevates the entire event — content their sponsors can use, coverage their exhibitors are proud of, and assets that prove the show delivered value. They're thinking about sponsor satisfaction, exhibitor retention, and positioning their event as the one people come back to. This maps to ICP \#1: The External Operator and ICP \#2: The Internal Owner.

**Audience 2: The Exhibitor / Field Marketer (Secondary)** They've invested in a booth and want documentation that makes their presence look polished and professional — product displays, team interactions, demonstrations, and the energy around their space. They're thinking about content they can share with leadership, use in post-show reporting, and leverage for future events. This maps to ICP \#3: The ROI Operator.

**Audience 3: The Venue / Convention Partner (Tertiary)** They're recommending vendors to show organizers and want a partner they can mention confidently. This maps to ICP \#4: The Market Gatekeeper. (This audience is not addressed with dedicated sections — they are served by the overall professionalism and venue familiarity communicated throughout the page.)

**Key Differentiation (Internal — Do Not Display):** Corporate Event Coverage follows the narrative arc of an event — opening to close, stage to breakout. Trade-Show Media Services works the floor. It's faster-paced, more brand-focused, captures a wider variety of stakeholders, and produces content that serves the host, the exhibitors, and the attendees simultaneously. The FAQ section addresses this distinction directly.

**Strategic Cross-Sell Architecture:** This page naturally introduces two complementary services:

* **Event Video Systems™** — Highlight reels, on-the-floor testimonials, social content. Video is positioned as completion of the media system, not an add-on.  
* **Headshot Activation™** — The engagement driver that stops foot traffic and gives attendees a high-value takeaway. Positioned as the natural pairing for exhibitors who want their booth to be a destination.

These links appear naturally in the content and point to their respective service pages.
